<p><ul><li>AC Coupled Solar Battery</li><li>DC-Coupled Solar Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home solar energy storage battery market primarily features two types: AC-coupled and DC-coupled solar batteries. AC-coupled systems connect solar panels and batteries via an inverter, allowing easy integration with existing setups and offering flexibility in battery upgrades or replacements. In contrast, DC-coupled systems link batteries directly to solar panels, resulting in higher efficiency and reduced energy loss during conversion. Each type serves different homeowner needs, depending on system compatibility, energy management preferences, and installation complexity.</p></p>
