<p><p>In the Spring Loaded Pressure Safety Valve (PSV) market, two primary types exist: closed type and unclosed type. The closed type features a sealing mechanism that prevents fluid escape when not in operation, providing better containment and safety during normal conditions. Conversely, the unclosed type allows for continuous flow, ensuring rapid pressure release but potentially exposing the system to fluid loss. Each type serves specific applications, dictated by operational requirements, safety standards, and potential environmental impacts.</p></p>
