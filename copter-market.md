<p><strong>The Copter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-rotor Type</li><li>Coaxial Twin Rotor</li><li>Split Shaft Twin Rotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Single-rotor Type** copter features one main rotor for lift and a tail rotor for stability, offering high efficiency and long flight times, ideal for heavy payloads. The **Coaxial Twin Rotor** design uses two rotors mounted on the same axis, rotating in opposite directions, providing compactness and enhanced maneuverability, suitable for confined spaces. The **Split Shaft Twin Rotor** employs two rotors on separate shafts, synchronized for balanced lift and stability, excelling in precision tasks and heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs.</p></p>
