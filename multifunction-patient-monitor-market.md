<p><p>The multifunction patient monitor market includes various types of devices designed for continuous patient monitoring. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices capture heart electrical activity, while event monitors allow for intermittent ECG recording to identify arrhythmias. Implantable Loop Recorders (ILRs) provide long-term cardiac data through a device implanted under the skin. Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) devices measure the heart's output, crucial for assessing cardiovascular health. Other devices in this market may include pulse oximeters and blood pressure monitors, enhancing overall patient care.</p></p>
