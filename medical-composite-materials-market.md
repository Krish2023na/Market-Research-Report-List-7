<p><p>The medical composite materials market encompasses three main types: Fiber Composites, Polymer-Metal Composites, and Polymer-Ceramic Composites. Fiber composites utilize reinforcing fibers, enhancing mechanical strength and flexibility, ideal for implants and prosthetics. Polymer-metal composites combine lightweight polymers with metals to create durable, corrosion-resistant materials suited for surgical instruments. Polymer-ceramic composites offer biocompatibility and mechanical properties, making them suitable for dental applications and bone repair. Each type contributes uniquely to advanced medical applications, enhancing patient outcomes and functionality.</p></p>
