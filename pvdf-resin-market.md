<p><ul><li>PVDF Granule</li><li>PVDF Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVDF resin market primarily consists of two main types: PVDF granules and PVDF powder. PVDF granules are often used in applications requiring substantial mechanical strength, flexibility, and processing efficiency, particularly in injection molding. Conversely, PVDF powder is favored for its superior flow properties and can be utilized in coatings, films, and membranes. Both forms are valued for their excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability, making them suitable for various industries, including chemicals, electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
