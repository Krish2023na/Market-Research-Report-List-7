<p><p>Xylo-oligosaccharides (XOS) are a group of carbohydrates derived from xylan, a polysaccharide found in the cell walls of plants. Due to their health benefits, XOS are gaining popularity in functional foods, dietary supplements, and animal feed.</p><p>The Xylo-oligosaccharide market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing consumer awareness of gut health, nutritional benefits, and the rising demand for natural and functional ingredients in food products.
