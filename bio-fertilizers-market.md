<p><ul><li>Nitrogen-Fixing</li><li>Phosphate-Solubilizing</li><li>Potash-mobilizing Bio Fertilizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biofertilizer market is categorized into three main types based on their functions. Nitrogen-fixing biofertilizers enrich soil by converting atmospheric nitrogen into forms plants can use, enhancing crop yield. Phosphate-solubilizing biofertilizers help make phosphorus more accessible to plants, promoting root development and overall growth. Potash-mobilizing biofertilizers increase potassium availability in the soil, essential for various plant physiological processes. Together, these biofertilizers contribute to sustainable agriculture by improving soil fertility and reducing chemical fertilizer dependence.</p></p>
