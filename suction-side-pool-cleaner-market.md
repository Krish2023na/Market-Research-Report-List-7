<p><p>The suction-side pool cleaner market includes two main types: wheeled and wheel-less cleaners. Wheeled cleaners utilize rotating wheels to navigate pool surfaces, providing effective cleaning by maneuvering over various obstacles and adapting to different pool shapes. In contrast, wheel-less cleaners rely on suction and water flow to glide smoothly across the pool floor without wheels, making them less prone to mechanical issues and easier to maintain. Both types offer distinct advantages in efficiency and cleaning capabilities for pool owners.</p></p>
