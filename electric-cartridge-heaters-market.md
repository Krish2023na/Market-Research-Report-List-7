<p><p>Electric cartridge heaters are commonly categorized into two market types: high temperature and standard. High temperature cartridge heaters are designed to operate at elevated temperatures, often exceeding 1000°F (537°C), making them ideal for applications like plastic molding, aerospace, and metal processing. Standard cartridge heaters, on the other hand, typically operate within lower temperature ranges, suitable for general heating tasks in electronics, packaging, and food equipment. Both types contribute significantly to efficient heat management across various industries.</p></p>
