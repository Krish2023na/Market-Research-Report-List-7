<p><p>The PE-RT market consists of polyethylene that has been modified for improved thermal resistance, categorized into three primary types based on their co-monomer: Ethylene-Octene, Ethylene-Hexene, and Ethylene-Butene. Ethylene-Octene offers superior flexibility and impact strength, making it ideal for plumbing and heating applications. Ethylene-Hexene provides enhanced transparency and processing ease, suitable for film and packaging. Ethylene-Butene balances performance and cost, often used in more general applications, reflecting a versatile range for various industries.</p></p>
