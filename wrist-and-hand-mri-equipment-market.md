<p><p>The wrist and hand MRI equipment market is categorized into three main types based on magnetic field strength. Low Field MRI (0.2T-0.3T) offers cost-effective imaging with less sensitivity to motion but lower resolution. Mid-Field MRI (0.5T-1.0T) strikes a balance between performance and cost, providing improved image quality while remaining affordable. High Field MRI (1.5T and above) delivers superior resolution and detail, making it ideal for precise assessments, but comes with higher operational costs and requires more specialized facilities.</p></p>
