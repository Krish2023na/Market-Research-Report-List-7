<p><strong>The Dye Sub Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ReTransfer</li><li>Direct to Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dye sub printers are primarily categorized into two market types: retransfer and direct-to-card. Retransfer printers print images onto a plastic film, which is then heated and pressed onto the card surface, offering superior image quality and durability. In contrast, direct-to-card printers print directly on the card surface, making them faster and more cost-effective for smaller runs. Each type serves different needs, with retransfer ideal for high-volume, high-quality applications and direct-to-card better suited for immediate printing tasks.</p></p>
