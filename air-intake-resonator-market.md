<p><p>The Air Intake Resonator market includes **In-Line Resonators** and **Side Branch Resonators**, both designed to reduce noise and optimize airflow in vehicle engines. **In-Line Resonators** are integrated directly into the air intake system, minimizing engine noise by canceling sound waves within the intake path. **Side Branch Resonators** are attached as separate chambers, targeting specific frequencies to dampen noise. Both types enhance engine performance and acoustic comfort, catering to different vehicle designs and noise reduction requirements.</p></p>
