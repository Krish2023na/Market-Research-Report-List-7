<p><p>Transformer oil coolers are essential for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in electrical transformers. The market is primarily segmented into air-cooled and water-cooled types. Air-cooled coolers use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for locations with limited water supply. In contrast, water-cooled coolers utilize circulating water for enhanced heat exchange, offering more efficient cooling in high-load situations. Both types are crucial for preventing overheating, ensuring the reliability and longevity of transformer operations in various industrial applications.</p></p>
