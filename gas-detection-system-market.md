<p><p>The gas detection system market features several key types: Semiconductor sensors use conductive materials that change resistance in the presence of gases. Electrochemical sensors detect specific gases through chemical reactions, providing high sensitivity. Catalytic combustion sensors measure flammable gases by igniting them and detecting the resultant heat. Photoionization (PID) sensors use ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, offering rapid detection of volatile organic compounds. Infrared sensors detect gases by measuring absorption of infrared light, providing a non-invasive and reliable measurement method.</p></p>
