<p><p>The electrical and electronic adhesive tape market is categorized into three main types: solvent-based, hot-melt based, and water-based tapes. Solvent-based tapes offer strong adhesion and durability, suitable for various environmental conditions. Hot-melt based tapes provide quick bonding with high initial tack, ideal for applications requiring immediate adhesion. Water-based tapes are eco-friendly, with lower emissions, making them a safer choice for many industries. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
