<p><p>The methanol catalysts market is primarily segmented into two types: methanol synthesis catalysts and methanol reforming catalysts. Methanol synthesis catalysts are used to convert carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide, and hydrogen into methanol, a crucial process in methanol production. Conversely, methanol reforming catalysts facilitate the conversion of methanol into hydrogen and other hydrocarbons, typically used in fuel cells and other energy applications. Both types are essential for optimizing efficiency and selectivity in their respective chemical processes.</p></p>
