<p><strong>The EPDM Foam Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Rubber</li><li>Semi-closed Cell Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EPDM foam rubber market is categorized into two main types: closed cell rubber and semi-closed cell rubber. Closed cell rubber features a structure where the cells are completely enclosed, providing excellent insulation, moisture resistance, and durability, making it ideal for applications in HVAC and automotive seals. Semi-closed cell rubber, on the other hand, has partially open cells, offering better flexibility and cushioning properties, suitable for applications like padding, gaskets, and soundproofing. Both types serve distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
