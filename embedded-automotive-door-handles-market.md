They offer a tactile, user-friendly experience. **Induction Type** handles use proximity sensors to detect a user's hand, automatically unlocking the door without physical contact. Both types prioritize convenience, security, and a futuristic appeal, catering to evolving automotive design trends and consumer preferences for advanced, minimalist vehicle exteriors.</p></p>
