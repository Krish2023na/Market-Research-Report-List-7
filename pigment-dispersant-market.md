<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pigment Dispersant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nonionic Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li><li>Anionic Type</li><li>Amphoteric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pigment Dispersant Market includes four types based on molecular charge: Nonionic, Cationic, Anionic, and Amphoteric. Nonionic dispersants are neutral, offering stability without charge interactions, ideal for non-polar systems. Cationic types carry a positive charge, suitable for positively charged pigments and acidic environments. Anionic dispersants have a negative charge, effective for negatively charged pigments and alkaline conditions. Amphoteric dispersants possess both positive and negative charges, providing versatility across varying pH levels and complex pigment systems. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
