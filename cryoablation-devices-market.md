<p><p>Cryoablation devices encompass various types designed for specific medical applications. Tissue contact probe ablator systems provide targeted cooling by directly applying the probe to the tissue, ensuring precise treatment. Tissue spray probe ablator systems utilize a spray mechanism to deliver cryogenic agents over larger areas, facilitating broader treatment zones. Epidermal and subcutaneous cryoablation systems are designed for superficial tissues, effectively treating conditions like skin lesions by delivering localized freezing. Each type is tailored for optimal efficacy and safety in cryotherapy procedures.</p></p>
