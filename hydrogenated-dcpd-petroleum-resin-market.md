<p><p>The Hydrogenated DCPD Petroleum Resin market is primarily divided into two types: monomer and copolymer. Monomer resins are derived from single Dicyclopentadiene units, offering unique properties suited for specific applications like adhesives and coatings. Copolymer resins, on the other hand, are formed by the combination of DCPD with other monomers, resulting in enhanced characteristics such as improved flexibility and durability. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and packaging, optimizing performance and functionality for various end uses.</p></p>
