<p><p>Vertical-axis wind turbines (VAWTs) are a type of wind turbine where the axis of rotation is vertical, as opposed to the traditional horizontal-axis wind turbines (HAWTs). This design allows VAWTs to capture wind from any direction, making them particularly suitable for urban settings and locations with turbulent wind patterns. Their compact size and lower center of gravity enhance structural stability and reduce installation and maintenance challenges.</p><p>The VAWT market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing focus on renewable energy sources and the demand for sustainable power generation solutions. Additionally, there is a growing interest in hybrid systems that combine VAWTs with solar energy solutions to optimize energy output.
