<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic chucks market primarily includes two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type chucks utilize electrostatic forces to hold semiconductor wafers during processing, providing high grip and stability. In contrast, JR Type chucks employ a combination of electrostatic and vacuum forces, enhancing grip while reducing wear on the wafer surface. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, each offering unique advantages suited for specific applications and operational requirements in the industry.</p></p>
