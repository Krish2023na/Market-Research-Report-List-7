<p><p>The glass bending furnaces market can be categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical type furnaces have a design that allows glass sheets to be stacked vertically, optimizing space and energy use, while ensuring uniform heating and bending. In contrast, horizontal type furnaces facilitate smoother handling of larger glass sheets, suitable for bulk production. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, influencing efficiency, production capacity, and the specific applications of the bent glass in various industries.</p></p>
