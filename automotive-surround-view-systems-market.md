<p><p>Automotive Surround View Systems (SVS) are advanced driver-assistance systems that provide a 360-degree view of the vehicle's surroundings using multiple cameras strategically placed around the car. These systems enhance safety by eliminating blind spots, aiding in parking, and improving overall situational awareness. The technology processes real-time images from the cameras to create a composite bird's-eye view, displayed on the vehicle's infotainment screen.</p><p>The Automotive Surround View Systems Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for advanced safety features, rising vehicle production, and stricter government regulations on vehicle safety. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.6% during the forecast period. The Asia-Pacific region is emerging as a major market due to rapid urbanization and increasing vehicle sales.</p></p>
