<p><strong>The Plate Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plate Reader market is primarily segmented into two types: Optical Filter Microplate Readers and Optical Grating Microplate Readers. Optical Filter Microplate Readers utilize specific wavelength filters for excitation and emission, making them ideal for straightforward applications like fluorescence assays. In contrast, Optical Grating Microplate Readers employ diffraction gratings to separate wavelengths, allowing for more versatility and high-resolution spectral detection. Both types cater to various research and diagnostic needs, enhancing the efficiency of biochemical and cellular assays.</p></p>
