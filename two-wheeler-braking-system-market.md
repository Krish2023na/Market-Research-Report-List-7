<p><ul><li>ABS Braking System</li><li>CBS Braking System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler braking system market is primarily categorized into two types: Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and Combined Braking System (CBS). ABS enhances safety by preventing wheel lock-up during braking, improving control and stability, particularly in slippery conditions. In contrast, CBS ensures that both brakes are applied simultaneously when either hand lever is used, promoting balanced braking and reducing stopping distances. Both systems aim to enhance rider safety and performance across various two-wheeled vehicles.</p></p>
