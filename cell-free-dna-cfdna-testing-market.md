<p><p>The cell-free DNA (cfDNA) testing market encompasses several key types. Donor-derived cfDNA (ddcfDNA) is utilized in transplant medicine to monitor organ rejection by analyzing DNA from the donor. Circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) focuses on detecting genetic mutations associated with cancer, offering insights for diagnosis and treatment. Cell-free fetal DNA (cfDNA) is primarily used in non-invasive prenatal testing (NIPT) to assess fetal health and genetic conditions, providing crucial information during pregnancy without invasive procedures.</p></p>
