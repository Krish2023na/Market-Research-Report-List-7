<p><strong>The 12-Inch Silicon Single Crystal Polished Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski Method</li><li>Float Zone Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 12-inch silicon single crystal polished wafer market encompasses two primary production methods: the Czochralski method and the Float Zone method. The Czochralski method involves melting silicon in a crucible and drawing up a crystal seed to form a large single crystal, suitable for mass production. In contrast, the Float Zone method uses a zoned molten region to refine the silicon crystal without contamination from crucibles, resulting in higher purity wafers. Both methods cater to various semiconductor applications, impacting the overall market dynamics.</p></p>
