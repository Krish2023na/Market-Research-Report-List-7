<p><p>Automated Dispensing Cabinets (ADCs) are categorized into three main types: Tablet ADCs, Dose ADCs, and Vial ADCs. Tablet ADCs are designed for the automated dispensing of solid oral medications, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. Dose ADCs manage and dispense pre-packaged doses, often personalized for patient-specific needs, ensuring proper medication administration. Vial ADCs focus on liquid medications stored in vials, allowing for secure and efficient access while maintaining inventory control. Together, these ADC types improve medication management in healthcare settings.</p></p>
