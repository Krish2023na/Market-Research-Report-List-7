<p><p>The lead-acid battery market is primarily categorized into two types: Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid (VRLA) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and designed for applications needing reliability and safety, making them ideal for backup power systems and renewable energy storage. In contrast, flooded batteries, which require regular maintenance and ventilation, offer higher capacity and longer lifespan, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse needs, driving advancements in energy storage solutions.</p></p>
