<p><p>Absolute optical encoders are precision devices that convert angular position into a digital signal using optical encoding technology. They provide a unique position value for each angle of rotation, allowing for accurate and reliable feedback in various applications such as robotics, industrial automation, and aerospace. <p><strong>The Absolute Optical Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-turn Encoder</li><li>Multi-turn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Absolute Optical Encoders market is divided into two main types: single-turn and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders capture position data within a single rotation, providing precise angular measurements typically up to 360 degrees. In contrast, multi-turn encoders track rotational position over multiple turns, often utilizing battery backup or gears, allowing for extended range and ensuring position retention during power loss. Both types are essential in applications requiring accurate motion control in various industries, including robotics and industrial automation.</p></p>
