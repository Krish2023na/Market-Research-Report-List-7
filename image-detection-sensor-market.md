<p><p>The image detection sensor market encompasses various types, including CCD, CMOS, and thermal sensors. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors are known for their high-quality image capture and low noise, making them suitable for professional photography. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors offer faster processing speeds and lower power consumption, widely used in consumer electronics. Thermal sensors detect infrared radiation, enabling temperature measurement and night vision applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in industries such as surveillance, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
