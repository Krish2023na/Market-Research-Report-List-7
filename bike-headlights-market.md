<p><p>The bike headlights market features two main types: self-generating headlights and battery-powered headlights. Self-generating headlights utilize energy from the bike's motion, converting kinetic energy into light, making them eco-friendly and maintenance-free. In contrast, battery-powered headlights rely on rechargeable or replaceable batteries to provide illumination. This type offers flexibility in brightness and features like adjustable settings but requires regular battery management. Both options cater to different rider preferences, focusing on sustainability and convenience in cycling safety.</p></p>
