<p><p>The full-size mobile C-arms market is divided into two main types: those utilizing pulsed fluoroscopy and those employing continuous fluoroscopy. Pulsed fluoroscopy provides intermittent images, reducing radiation exposure while maintaining image quality for procedures requiring less constant monitoring. In contrast, continuous fluoroscopy delivers a constant stream of images, allowing for real-time visualization during complex surgeries. Both types cater to different clinical needs, balancing radiation safety and imaging requirements for various medical applications in operating rooms and diagnostic settings.</p></p>
