<p><ul><li>Single-Frequency GNSS Receiver</li><li>Multi-GNSS Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS receivers market is primarily segmented into single-frequency and multi-GNSS receivers. Single-frequency GNSS receivers utilize signals from one satellite frequency, typically for basic positioning tasks, making them cost-effective but less accurate in challenging environments. In contrast, multi-GNSS receivers can access signals from various satellite systems, enhancing accuracy and reliability by leveraging multiple frequencies. This makes them suitable for demanding applications such as surveying, autonomous vehicles, and precision agriculture, driving their growing popularity and market demand.</p></p>
