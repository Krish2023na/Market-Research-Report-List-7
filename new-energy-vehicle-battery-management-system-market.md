<p><strong>The New Energy Vehicle Battery Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Battery Management System (BMS) market is categorized into three types: Distributed, Centralized, and Modular. Distributed BMS features individual control units for each battery cell, offering precise monitoring but higher complexity. Centralized BMS uses a single control unit for the entire battery pack, simplifying design but limiting scalability. Modular BMS combines both approaches, grouping cells into modules with localized control, balancing precision and scalability, making it adaptable for diverse NEV applications.</p></p>
