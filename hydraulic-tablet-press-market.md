<p><p>The hydraulic tablet press market consists of two primary types: single station and multi-station hydraulic tablet presses. Single station hydraulic tablet presses are designed for smaller production runs, allowing for the compression of powder into tablets in a single operation, ideal for R&D or small batch manufacturing. Multi-station hydraulic tablet presses, on the other hand, are equipped with multiple stations that allow for simultaneous compression of tablets, making them suitable for large-scale production, increasing efficiency and output.</p></p>
