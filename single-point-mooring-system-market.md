<p><strong>The Single Point Mooring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA)</li><li>Vertical Load Anchors (VLA)</li><li>Suction Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Point Mooring (SPM) systems utilize various anchor types to secure floating vessels in offshore operations. Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA) work by embedding into the seabed through lateral drag, providing stability under dynamic loads. Vertical Load Anchors (VLA) rely on vertical forces and are often used in deeper waters. Suction Anchors utilize negative pressure to embed into the seabed, offering high holding capacity. Each type caters to specific environmental conditions, enhancing the versatility and reliability of SPM systems.</p></p>
