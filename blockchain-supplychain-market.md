<p><p>Blockchain supply chain markets can be categorized into three types: Public, Private, and Consortium Blockchains. Public Blockchains are open to anyone, enhancing transparency and trust among all stakeholders, suitable for decentralized supply chains. Private Blockchains, restricted to specific organizations, focus on data privacy and control, enhancing efficiency within a single company or tightly-knit group. Consortium Blockchains combine both, allowing multiple organizations to collaborate while maintaining some level of access control, thus balancing transparency and privacy in shared supply chains.</p></p>
