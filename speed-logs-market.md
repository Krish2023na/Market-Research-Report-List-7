<p><p>Speed logs are critical for maritime navigation, measuring vessel speed through various technologies. Electromagnetic speed logs use electromagnetic fields to gauge water movement, while Doppler speed logs employ the Doppler effect for accurate speed calculations. Pitometer logs measure speed by analyzing pressure differences, and impeller logs utilize a spinning rotor to determine speed based on water flow. GPS speed logs leverage satellite signals to calculate position and speed over ground, offering precise, real-time data essential for safe navigation and operational efficiency.</p></p>
