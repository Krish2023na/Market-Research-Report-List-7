Polymer coatings are flexible and resistant to corrosion, commonly used to protect wind turbine components from environmental damage. Ceramic coatings provide high durability and thermal resistance, making them suitable for high-stress applications. Metal coatings, often applied for their strength and protective qualities, enhance the longevity of turbine parts. Each type plays a crucial role in improving efficiency and reducing maintenance costs in wind energy systems.</p></p>
