<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Assisted Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPAS</li><li>P-EPAS</li><li>R-EPAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) systems are categorized into three main types: C-EPAS, P-EPAS, and R-EPAS. C-EPAS features a compact motor directly assisting the steering column, enhancing efficiency and reducing weight. P-EPAS uses a motor mounted on the steering gear, providing customizable assistance based on driving conditions for improved performance. R-EPAS is designed for more responsive handling, with a motor integrated into the rack, allowing for variable assistance and better feedback from the road. These systems enhance vehicle maneuverability and driver comfort.</p></p>
