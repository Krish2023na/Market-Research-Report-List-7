<p><ul><li>Primary Fatty Amine</li><li>Secondary Fatty Amine</li><li>Tertiary Fatty Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fatty nitrogen compounds market is categorized into three main types: primary, secondary, and tertiary fatty amines. Primary fatty amines contain one nitrogen atom bonded to one alkyl chain, making them ideal for use in surfactants and emulsifiers. Secondary fatty amines have two alkyl chains attached to the nitrogen, enhancing their solubility and versatility in solvents. Tertiary fatty amines, with three alkyl groups attached, exhibit unique properties suited for applications in personal care, detergents, and agricultural chemicals.</p></p>
