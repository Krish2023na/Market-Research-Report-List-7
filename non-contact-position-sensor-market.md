<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-contact position sensor market is primarily categorized into linear and rotary position sensors. Linear position sensors measure displacement along a straight path, typically using technologies like magnetism or capacitance, making them ideal for applications in automation and robotics. Rotary position sensors, on the other hand, track angular displacement, often utilizing optical or magnetic methods. Both types offer high precision, durability, and reduced wear, making them crucial in industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for accurate position measurement without physical contact.</p></p>
