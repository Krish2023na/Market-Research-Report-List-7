<p><strong>The Biological Crop Protection (Bio-Pesticide) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbial Pesticides</li><li>Biochemical Pesticides</li><li>Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Biological Crop Protection (Bio-Pesticide) market includes eco-friendly alternatives to chemical pesticides, categorized into four main types. **Microbial Pesticides** use microorganisms like bacteria, fungi, or viruses to control pests. **Biochemical Pesticides** employ natural substances, such as pheromones or plant extracts, to disrupt pest behavior or growth. **Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs)** are genetically modified crops that produce pest-resistant proteins. **Others** include bio-nematicides or bio-herbicides. These solutions promote sustainable agriculture by reducing chemical residues, enhancing soil health, and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
