<p><p>The industrial dyes market comprises various types of dyes catering to specific applications. Disperse dyes are used primarily for synthetic fibers, providing vibrant color without impacting the material. Reactive dyes bond chemically with fibers, ideal for cotton and other cellulosic fabrics. Sulfur dyes offer durability and are commonly used for dark colors on cotton. Vat dyes are known for their excellent wash fastness and are employed in high-quality applications. Acid dyes are suitable for protein fibers, while other dyes include specific formulations and specialty inks.</p></p>
