<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Cutting Machine</li><li>Fiber Laser Cutting Machine</li><li>YAG Cutting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser tube cutting machines are categorized into three main types: CO2 laser cutting machines, fiber laser cutting machines, and YAG cutting machines. CO2 machines utilize gas lasers, offering high-quality cuts on various materials but are typically slower. Fiber laser cutting machines leverage solid-state technology, allowing faster cutting speeds and efficiency, particularly on metals. YAG cutting machines use solid-state lasers, suitable for applications requiring high precision, often in smaller-scale production. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on material and cutting requirements.</p></p>
