<p><strong>The Laboratory Drying Ovens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forced Air and Vacuum Ovens</li><li>Gravity Convection Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory drying ovens are essential for removing moisture from materials. Forced air ovens utilize fans to circulate heated air, ensuring uniform temperature and efficient drying. Vacuum ovens operate under reduced pressure, allowing for lower temperature drying, which is ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Gravity convection ovens rely on natural air circulation, providing a more gentle drying process but may have less uniform temperature distribution. Each type caters to specific laboratory needs, balancing efficiency, temperature control, and material compatibility.</p></p>
