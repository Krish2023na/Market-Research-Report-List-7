<p><strong>The PV Inverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>String Inverter</li><li>Central Inverter</li><li>Microinverters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV inverter market consists of three main types: string inverters, central inverters, and microinverters. String inverters connect multiple solar panels in a series, making them cost-effective for residential and small commercial systems. Central inverters are larger, designed to manage energy from multiple strings, suitable for utility-scale projects. Microinverters are attached to individual panels, optimizing performance and providing detailed monitoring, making them ideal for complex roof layouts and maximizing energy generation in smaller installations.</p></p>
